What Types of Disrepair Can You Claim For?

Common problems reported in Adur properties include:

  • Black mould and condensation in bedrooms or bathrooms
  • Persistent damp patches or water damage
  • Cracked walls or ceilings
  • Broken boilers or unsafe heating systems
  • Leaking roofs or guttering
  • Faulty electrics or plumbing

If you've reported these issues and the council hasn’t taken action within a reasonable timeframe, you may be eligible to make a no win, no fee housing disrepair claim.

Health Issues Caused by Housing Disrepair

Living in poor housing conditions isn't just unpleasant — it can be dangerous. We regularly speak to tenants in Adur suffering from:

  • Worsened asthma or breathing issues due to mould
  • Infections and skin conditions
  • Stress, anxiety, and sleep deprivation

Your landlord has a legal responsibility to maintain a safe and healthy home. If they fail, you could claim for:

  • Medical impact
  • Damage to belongings
  • Inconvenience and hardship
