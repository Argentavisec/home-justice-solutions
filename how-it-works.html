How the Housing Disrepair Claim Process Works

At Home Justice Solutions, we make it simple to get help with unsafe or damaged council or housing association properties. Our online process is designed to be fast, stress-free, and completely free to start.

Whether you're dealing with damp, mould, leaks, pests, broken heating, or dangerous electrics — here’s how it works from start to finish.

Step 1: Complete a Free Online Form

Click the “Check If You Qualify” button and answer a few quick questions about your property, repairs, and landlord. This takes less than 60 seconds and doesn’t require any phone calls.

Start Your Free Check

Step 2: Speak to a Housing Disrepair Expert

If your claim meets the requirements, you'll be connected with a regulated housing disrepair solicitor. They’ll confirm the details, review your evidence (such as photos or emails), and assess how strong your case is.

Step 3: Repairs and Compensation Begin

If your claim is accepted, your solicitor will begin legal proceedings to force your landlord to make the repairs. You may also receive financial compensation for the inconvenience and harm caused.

Common awards range between £1,000 and £10,000+, depending on how long you’ve suffered and how serious the issues are.

Step 4: No Win, No Fee Protection

All claims are handled on a no win, no fee basis — meaning there’s nothing to pay upfront. You only pay if the case wins, and that cost comes from the compensation.

Is This Legit?

Yes — your claim is handled by experienced, SRA-regulated solicitors who specialise in council and housing association disrepair cases. You’re legally protected as a tenant, and your landlord has a duty to fix health hazards once notified.
