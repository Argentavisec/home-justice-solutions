Aberdeen City Council Housing Disrepair Claims

If you're a tenant living in an Aberdeen City Council property and suffering due to issues like persistent damp, mould, leaks, or structural problems, you may be entitled to compensation. At Home Justice Solutions, we help tenants across Aberdeen make successful claims against councils who fail in their duty to maintain safe and habitable homes.

What Can I Claim For?

Many tenants in Aberdeen face unacceptable living conditions — from black mould growing in bathrooms, leaking ceilings that damage belongings, to cold, unsafe homes due to faulty heating. You can potentially claim for:

  • Health problems caused by disrepair (e.g. asthma from mould)
  • Damage to furniture or possessions
  • Inconvenience, stress, or moving expenses
