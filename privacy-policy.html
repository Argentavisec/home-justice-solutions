Privacy Policy

This privacy policy outlines how Home Justice Solutions collects, uses, and protects your personal information. By using our website, you agree to the terms of this policy.

1. What We Collect

We may collect the following information when you use our site or fill out a claim form:

  • Full name
  • Contact information including phone number and email address
  • Address and housing details
  • Details about your housing disrepair issue

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide to:

  • Assess your eligibility for a housing disrepair claim
  • Connect you with our trusted legal or claims partners
  • Communicate with you regarding your enquiry
  • Improve our services and website experience

3. Data Protection & Storage

We take the protection of your data seriously. Your information is stored securely and only shared with third parties where necessary to progress your enquiry.

We comply with the UK GDPR and Data Protection Act 2018.

4. Sharing Your Information

We may share your data with carefully vetted legal firms or housing disrepair specialists who can help with your case. We do not sell your data to any unrelated third parties.

5. Cookies & Analytics

This website uses cookies to improve user experience and gather anonymised analytics data. You can disable cookies in your browser settings if you prefer not to allow this.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request a copy of your personal data
  • Ask for data to be corrected or deleted
  • Withdraw consent at any time

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Any major changes will be posted here with a revision date.

Disclaimer: Home Justice Solutions is not affiliated with any council. We do not offer legal advice. We help connect tenants to relevant claims professionals.
