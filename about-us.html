Our Debt Solutions

At Fresh Start Debt Support, we offer a wide range of debt solutions designed to fit your needs, whether you have credit card debt, personal loans, payday loans, council tax arrears, or other unsecured debts. Our main services include:

  • Individual Voluntary Arrangements (IVA): A formal insolvency agreement allowing you to repay a portion of your debt over a fixed term, typically five years, while protecting your assets.
  • Debt Management Plans (DMP): An informal agreement to make reduced monthly payments to your creditors through a debt management company.
  • Debt Consolidation: Combining multiple debts into one manageable monthly payment, often at a lower interest rate.
  • Bankruptcy Advice: Guidance on declaring bankruptcy when other solutions are not viable, including the consequences and benefits.
  • Trust Deeds (Scotland): A legal debt solution for Scottish residents similar to an IVA.

Our debt advisors carefully assess your financial situation to recommend the most appropriate option and guide you through the application and management process.
