About Home Justice Solutions

At Home Justice Solutions, we believe every tenant deserves a safe, secure and habitable home. That’s why we work with tenants across the UK to help them get free repairs and compensation for housing disrepair — including mould, leaks, damp, and heating issues.

Whether you rent from the council or a housing association, if your landlord hasn’t resolved reported issues, we’re here to help you take action quickly and easily.

Who We Help

We support council tenants and housing association tenants throughout England, Wales and Scotland. No matter where you’re based, if your landlord has failed to fix key issues in your home, we can help you explore a claim — at no cost to you.

We also work with people affected by:

  • Persistent black mould and condensation
  • Leaks and flooding
  • Structural disrepair (walls, ceilings, stairs)
  • Rodents, pests, damp and unsafe electrics
  • Lack of heating or broken boiler
