üè† What issues can I claim for?

There are many types of disrepair that may qualify for legal action. These include:

  • Black mould or rising damp
  • Leaks from pipes, ceilings or roofs
  • Broken or faulty heating and hot water systems
  • Infestations of mice, rats or cockroaches
  • Exposed electrics or broken light fixtures
  • Cracks in walls, ceilings or broken windows
